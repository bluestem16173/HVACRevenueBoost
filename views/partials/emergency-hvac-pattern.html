Important Homeowner Warning

STOP! Don't keep running a struggling AC.

If your AC is running but not cooling, continuing to run the system can cause a $1,500+ compressor failure or freeze the indoor coils completely.

Turn the thermostat OFF immediately if:

  • Air from vents feels warm or room temperature
  • The outdoor unit is making a strange buzzing or screeching noise
  • You see ice building up on the indoor coil or the outdoor copper line
  • The circuit breaker has tripped more than once

If you're unsure, request emergency local HVAC diagnostic service.
