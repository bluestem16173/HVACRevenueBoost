## Tailwind Classes

| Semantic | Class | Hex |
|----------|-------|-----|
| Primary dark / headings | `hvac-navy` | #0a192f |
| Links, accents | `hvac-blue` | #1e3a8a |
| CTA, success, DIY-friendly | `hvac-gold` | #d4af37 |
